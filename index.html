Privacy and analytics transparency

Ice Fishing Stats Tracker may use Firebase Analytics and AppsFlyer to process limited usage data, app activity, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app version, device type, and diagnostic information.

This data may be used for analytics, attribution, app improvement, marketing measurement, fraud prevention, security, and compliance. Data is transmitted using secure protocols such as HTTPS/TLS.

The app does not currently provide a direct in-app mechanism or separate request process for users to request deletion of analytics, attribution, or device identifier data. For full details, please review the Privacy Policy.
