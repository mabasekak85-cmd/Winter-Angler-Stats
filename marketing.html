Built with focus, learning, and long-term value.

Ice Fishing Stats Tracker is developed by Bedsted Dev, an early-stage independent developer account. The goal is to create a useful, honest, and practical tool for people who enjoy ice fishing and want a simple way to track their fishing activity.

The project is still growing. At the current stage, the app does not include paid features or subscriptions. In the future, paid functionality or subscriptions may be introduced to support continued development, maintenance, and improvement of the app.

What the App Aims to Provide

🎣 Practical tracking

Help users keep notes and statistics from ice fishing sessions in a clear and organized way.

📈 Better personal insight

Support users in understanding their own fishing activity over time through simple records.

🧊 Focused experience

Keep the app focused on one clear purpose instead of adding unnecessary complexity.

🔐 Transparent approach

Provide clear support, privacy, and developer information for both users and reviewers.

Development Roadmap

Current stage: Build a reliable, simple app foundation for tracking ice fishing activity.
Next improvements: Improve usability, polish the interface, and respond to user feedback.
Future plan: Consider optional paid features or subscriptions only after there is clear value for users.
