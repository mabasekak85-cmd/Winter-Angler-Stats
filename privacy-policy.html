1. Information We Collect

Ice Fishing Stats Tracker may collect limited non-personal and technical data through third-party analytics and attribution services, including Firebase Analytics and AppsFlyer.

The types of data that may be collected include:

  • App activity and usage data: app launches, screen views, sessions, session duration, interactions with app features, and in-app events.
  • Device or other identifiers: advertising identifiers, app instance identifiers, device identifiers, or similar technical identifiers.
  • Approximate location: approximate location may be derived from technical information such as IP address for analytics, attribution, fraud prevention, regional reporting, and marketing measurement.
  • Technical data: device type, operating system version, app version, language, country or region, and diagnostic information.

2. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

We do not intentionally collect directly identifying personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, payment information, or contact information through the app.

3. How We Use Information

Collected data may be used for:

  • Analytics and measurement of app usage;
  • Understanding how users interact with the app;
  • Improving app performance, stability, and user experience;
  • Attribution and measurement of advertising or marketing campaigns;
  • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

4. Data Storage

We do not store user data on our own developer-controlled servers. However, data collected through Firebase Analytics and AppsFlyer may be stored and processed by those third-party service providers according to their own policies, retention rules, and applicable legal requirements.

Any fishing notes, catch records, or session information that a user may enter into the app is intended to remain under the user’s control on the user’s device, unless the app interface or operating system clearly states otherwise.

5. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

The app may use third-party services including Firebase Analytics by Google and AppsFlyer. These services may collect, receive, process, and store usage data, app activity data, device identifiers, approximate location, and technical data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Data may be shared with or processed by third-party analytics, attribution, advertising, marketing measurement, and fraud prevention providers, including Firebase Analytics and AppsFlyer.

We do not sell personal information. However, certain data may be processed by third-party service providers as described in this Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security

Data transmitted from the app to third-party services is encrypted in transit using secure protocols such as HTTPS/TLS. Although reasonable security measures are used, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

7. User Data Deletion

At this time, Ice Fishing Stats Tracker does not provide a direct in-app mechanism or separate request process for users to request deletion of analytics, attribution, or device identifier data.

Accordingly, in the Google Play Data Safety section, the answer to whether users can request data deletion is: No.

8. Children’s Privacy

Ice Fishing Stats Tracker is not intended to knowingly collect directly identifying personal information from children. The app may collect limited usage data, app activity data, device identifiers, approximate location, and technical data through third-party analytics and attribution services as described above.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

9. Future Changes

If future versions of the app introduce additional features that affect data collection, paid functionality, subscriptions, cloud sync, analytics, or third-party services, this Privacy Policy will be updated clearly.
